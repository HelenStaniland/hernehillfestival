Peckham Rye Sings

Peckham Rye Sings is a vibrant and friendly community choir based in Peckham and led by local choir leader Xenia Davis. They sing joyful and uplifting songs, from pop arrangements to lullabies, and from countries spanning the British Isles, southern Africa, eastern Europe, north America and Georgia. All their songs are learnt by ear. They love the occasional flashmob, and are well known for audience participation. The choir meets on Monday evenings from 7.30-9pm at the┬ĀCopleston Centre, and this year are celebrating their 10th birthday.

"Peckham Rye Sings (perform) beautiful a cappella folk, gospel and world music songs in 3 and 4 part harmony. All songs are taught by ear." (naturalvoice.net)

Palace Acappella with Beckenham Acapella Choir

Palace Acappella joins new sister choir Beckenham Acappella to perform together at Herne Hill Sings On. Both choirs are run by local choir leader Eleanor Rastall, sharing core aims and values while maintaining their own individual identities. Both are non-auditioning community choirs, offer free taster sessions, and value social connection, collaboration and a supportive space to find and develop your voice and skills. In April this year Beckenham Acappella launched and Palace Acappella celebrated its 6th birthday! They are excited to perform together in the Herne Hill Festival, Beckenham Acappella for the first time.
