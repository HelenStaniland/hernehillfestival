Misty & Rufus Miller

Brockwell Park Community Greenhouses

Doors open 30mins before the performance.

Misty and Rufus Miller are two South London musicians who also happen to be brother and sister. They perform as solo artists independently as well as performing with other artists. Both have a a long career of wowing audiences with their soulful voices and on this special concert they will come together to play songs from their back catalogue as well as some of their favourite music.

They are very excited to play Herne Hill Music Festival and look forward to showcasing the music that they don't always get to do, making this a very special event. Expect to hear folk, country, Americana, raw confessional music. A moving show and a wholesome time as brother and sister share the stage together.

Misty & Rufus Miller

Sister & Brother Duo Misty & Rufus Miller have been performing locally in South London for over a decade. Rufus under his own name with his own band and also earlier some may remember in his POPSKULL persona. Up until a few years ago Rufus was also travelling the world as part of STING's touring band as second guitarist with his father Dominic Miller, Sting's main collaborator for 30 odd years.

Misty is Rufus's younger sister and has been performing from a very early age, having a number of recording deals along the way. She has fronted many of her own band incarnations and recently was chosen to perform songs for the Amazon Original Movie 'Catherine Called Birdy' soundtrack. She deserves your attention.

Both are prolific singer-songwriters, their sibling vocal harmonies are sublime, HHMF are proud to have them performing a unique set at Brockwell Park Community Greenhouses.
