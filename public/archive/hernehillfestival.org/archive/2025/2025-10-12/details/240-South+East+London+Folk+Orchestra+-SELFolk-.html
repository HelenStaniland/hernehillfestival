South East London Folk Orchestra

Fiddles, accordions, concertinas, whistles, bodhrans, guitars, mandolins, flutes – the South East London Orchestra has it all! SelFolk  play tunes which are guaranteed to put a smile on your face and get your toes tapping, and will encourage you to dance and sing along!

The South East London Folk Orchestra was founded in 2012 and consists of local folk enthusiasts who meet together once a week in Forest Hill to play and sing tunes from a variety of folk traditions.

They perform regularly at local community events and especially enjoy encouraging their audience to sing along and to dance.

So, come along for an evening of great toe-tapping tunes, songs you can join in with, and some ceilidh dancing. Suitable for all ages!
