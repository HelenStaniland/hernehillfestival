Nunhead Community Choir

Nunhead Community Choir is an inclusive, non-audition, friendly choir who sing for pleasure. We sing mainly pop, rock, soul and traditional songs, the majority of which are arranged by our young and talented musical director Ben Swan. We meet on Thursday evenings at Ivydale School during term time, and perform at all sorts of local events. In 2024 we are excited to be celebrating our 15th anniversary!
