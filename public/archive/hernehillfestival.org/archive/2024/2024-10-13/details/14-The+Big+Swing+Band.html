The Big Swing Band

The Big Swing Band is based in Camberwell and has been entertaining audiences in the area and beyond since 1982.  They play a mixture of classic, swing and modern big band charts including repertoire written especially for the band.  They are led by alto saxophonist Alan English and have the standard 5-4-4-4 line up.

Friends of Ruskin Park

We are a volunteer-run charity which works to ensure the park (managed by Lambeth Council) is well cared for and maintained, and has a planned and healthy future. Since 1996 we’ve raised money to help maintain and improve the park, preserving it for future generations. We also organise and support a regular programme of events and activities.
