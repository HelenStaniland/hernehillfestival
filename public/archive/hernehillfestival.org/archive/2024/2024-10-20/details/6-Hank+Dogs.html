Hank Dogs

Legendary UK & South London Alt Country, folk roots and Americana band Hank Dogs are finally set to release their long-awaited third album around the time they play HHMF in the wonderful surroundings of our Greenhouse venue. Their first two albums were produced by Joe Boyd (Nick Drake, John Martin, Fairport Convention...) The album will be on sale at the gig.
Andy has been running The Easycome Acoustic Club in South London for over 30 years, it remains one of the longest running clubs in London.

Hank Dogs - Andy Allan, Piano and Lily Ramona - started out at folk clubs in London in the early 1990s becoming a vital part of the British modern folk scene. In 1998 they went worlwide when Nick Drake's legendary producer Joe Boyd declared them the first British act he'd loved in 30 years.
