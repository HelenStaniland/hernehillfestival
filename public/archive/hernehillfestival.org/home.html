Join the Herne Hill Music Festival 2026

Opportunity to join the Herne Hill Music Festival 2026 Committee

After the sold-out success of Herne Hill Music Festival 2025, we're on the lookout for volunteers to help us bring award-winning acts and community events back to SE24 this year!

If you're a music lover, a festival aficionado, or simply want to give something back to our South London community, we have plenty of opportunities for you. We're a community organisation, run by lovely volunteer members who meet about once a month in Herne Hill. Whether you're from Herne Hill or not, it's a fantastic way to play a part in the musical world or gain loads of events experience.

We have plenty of roles available, such as:
suggesting great talent to appear at this year's festival;
organising events involving the local community;
organising our classical music, jazz and world events;
liaising with our fantastic venues - or even suggesting more ones;
designing our press and marketing materials and liaising with our graphic designers;
giving out flyers each September at the Herne Hill Farmers' Market;
manning the ticket desks (a good way to see the events!);
and much more!
If you'd like to put your stamp on this year's Festival - or know someone who might! - the next Committee meeting will be at 7.30pm on Monday 9th February 2026 in Herne Hill. Drop Nicky an email at cellisti@btinternet.com and we'll be in touch with venue details.

Thank you!
