Festival wins Excellence Awards

The Festival won the Herne Hill Society Excellence Award 2023 in the Community category at the ceremony held in April. We were both astonished and delighted, and we would like to thank the judging panel for their decision.

The panel cited our contribution to bringing local community organisations and musicians together, raising the profile of Herne Hill as a place distinct from the neighbouring communities, providing good quality entertainment, and the great contribution made by our team of volunteers.
